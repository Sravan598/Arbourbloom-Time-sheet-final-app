## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

To join AurborBloom, you need an invitation code from your administrator.

1. Get your invitation code from your admin (format: `INV-XXXXXX`)
2. Go to the AurborBloom signup page
3. Enter your invitation code
4. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm Password
5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Logging In

1. Go to the AurborBloom login page
2. Make sure **"Employee"** tab is selected
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Sign in as Employee"**

### First Login

After your first login:
1. Complete your profile (add photo, contact info)
2. Familiarize yourself with the dashboard
3. Check your assigned projects

## Time Tracking

### Clocking In

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Find the **Clock In/Out Widget**
3. Click **"Clock In"**
4. Your start time is recorded

> 💡 **Tip**: Clock in when you start working each day.

### Clocking Out

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click **"Clock Out"**
3. Your end time and total hours are recorded

### Taking Breaks

1. Click **"Start Break"** when taking a break
2. Click **"End Break"** when returning
3. Break time is automatically deducted from work hours

### Work Status Indicators

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Clocked In | 🟢 Green | Currently working |
| On Break | 🟡 Yellow | On break |
| Clocked Out | ⚪ Gray | Not working |

## Viewing Timesheets

### Accessing Timesheets

1. Click **"Timesheet"** in the sidebar
2. View your daily time records

### Timesheet Information

Each entry shows:
- Date
- Clock in time
- Clock out time
- Break duration
- Total hours
- Status

### Timesheet Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Awaiting admin approval |
| **Approved** | Confirmed by admin |
| **Rejected** | Not approved (check notes) |

### Requesting Corrections

If you made a mistake:
1. Find the timesheet entry
2. Click **"Request Correction"**
3. Enter the correct times
4. Add a reason for the correction
5. Submit the request
6. Wait for admin approval

## Requesting Leave

### Submitting a Leave Request

1. Click **"Leave"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Request Leave"**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Leave Type**: Annual, Sick, Personal, Other
   - **Start Date**: First day of leave
   - **End Date**: Last day of leave
   - **Reason**: Brief explanation
4. Click **"Submit Request"**

### Leave Types

| Type | Description | Documentation |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Annual Leave** | Vacation time | Usually not required |
| **Sick Leave** | Medical reasons | May require doctor's note |
| **Personal Leave** | Personal matters | Brief reason required |
| **Other** | Special circumstances | Explanation required |

### Checking Leave Status

1. Go to **Leave** page
2. View your requests and their status:
   - **Pending**: Waiting for approval
   - **Approved**: Leave confirmed
   - **Rejected**: Not approved (see admin notes)

### Cancelling a Leave Request

1. Find the pending leave request
2. Click **"Cancel"**
3. Confirm cancellation

> ⚠️ Note: You can only cancel pending requests.

## Support Tickets

### Creating a Ticket

1. Click **"Tickets"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Ticket"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Subject**: Brief title
   - **Category**: IT, HR, Facilities, or Other
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
4. Optionally attach files
5. Click **"Submit"**

### Ticket Categories

| Category | Use For | Examples |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **IT** | Technical issues | Computer problems, software access |
| **HR** | HR matters | Policy questions, benefits |
| **Facilities** | Office needs | Desk setup, supplies |
| **Other** | General | Anything else |

### Priority Levels

| Priority | Response Time | Use When |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Low** | 3-5 days | Minor issues |
| **Medium** | 1-2 days | Standard requests |
| **High** | Same day | Impacting work |
| **Urgent** | ASAP | Critical blocker |

### Tracking Your Tickets

Your tickets appear in a Kanban board:

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Open** | Submitted, waiting for action |
| **In Progress** | Being worked on |
| **Resolved** | Issue fixed |
| **Closed** | Completed |

### Adding Comments

1. Click on your ticket
2. Scroll to comments section
3. Type your message
4. Attach files if needed
5. Click **"Add Comment"**

## FAQ

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
> Contact your administrator to reset your password.

**Q: My invitation code doesn't work.**
> Codes expire after 7 days and can only be used once. Ask your admin for a new code.

**Q: I'm locked out of my account.**
> Contact your administrator. Your account may be deactivated.

### Time Tracking

**Q: I forgot to clock in/out. What should I do?**
> Submit a correction request through your Timesheet page.

**Q: My hours look incorrect.**
> Check if break times were recorded. If still wrong, request a correction.

**Q: Do I need to clock in for remote work?**
> Yes, clock in/out regardless of location to track your hours.

### Leave

**Q: How far in advance should I request leave?**
> We recommend at least 2 weeks for planned leave, but emergencies are exceptions.

**Q: Can I change my leave dates after approval?**
> Cancel the current request and submit a new one.

**Q: How do I know if my leave was approved?**
> Check the Leave page or look for notifications.

### Tickets

**Q: How long until my ticket is addressed?**
> Depends on priority: Urgent (ASAP), High (same day), Medium (1-2 days), Low (3-5 days).

**Q: Can I close my own ticket?**
> No, only admins can change ticket status.

**Q: How do I add more information to my ticket?**
> Add a comment on the existing ticket.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The page won't load.**
> Try refreshing (Ctrl+F5) or clearing your browser cache.

**Q: I can't upload files.**
> Check file size limits and try a different file format.
