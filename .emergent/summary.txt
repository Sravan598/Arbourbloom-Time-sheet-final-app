<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user's initial goal was to build a premium, user-friendly marketing website and time-tracking application called CORtracker.

The scope has since expanded significantly based on user requests during this session. The current product requirements include:
1.  **Marketing Website:** A single-page, modern marketing site for CORtracker.
2.  **Authentication:** A secure JWT-based signup/login system with two roles: **ADMIN** and **EMPLOYEE**.
3.  **Employee Dashboard Features:**
    *   Clock In / Clock Out.
    *   **Leave/PTO Request System:** Employees can request time off and view balances.
    *   **Break Timer:** Employees can track lunch, coffee, and other breaks.
    *   **Weekly Hours Goal & Progress:** A visual progress bar showing hours worked against a weekly goal (e.g., 40 hours).
    *   **Export Timesheet to PDF:** Employees can download a professional PDF summary of their timesheet.
    *   **Overtime Alerts:** Employees receive warnings when approaching or exceeding their weekly hour goal.
    *   **Quick Notes:** Add optional notes when clocking in or out. (In Progress)
4.  **Admin Dashboard Features:**
    *   View all employees and their live status.
    *   Manage and edit all employee timesheets.
    *   **Leave Management:** Approve or reject employee leave requests.
    *   **Project Management:** Create projects, set details (color, hours), and assign them to employees.
    *   **Team Progress View:** An overview of all employees' weekly hour progress, including status indicators for who is on track, behind, or in overtime.
