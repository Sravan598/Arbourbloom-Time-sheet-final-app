**key api endpoints**
- **Chat:**
    - `GET /api/chat/init`: Initializes chat data.
    - `POST /api/chat/channels`: Creates a channel.
    - `POST /api/chat/channels/{channel_id}/messages`: Sends a message.
- **Leave & Notifications:**
    - `POST /api/leave/requests`: (Employee) Submits a leave request.
    - `GET /api/admin/leave/requests`: (Admin) Retrieves all leave requests.
    - `PUT /api/admin/leave/requests/{request_id}`: (Admin) Approves/denies a request.
    - `POST /api/admin/leave/types`: (Admin) Creates a new default leave type.
    - `GET /api/notifications`: Retrieves notifications for the logged-in user.
